How the ATS Score Works

1. Structure (30 points)

We check if your resume has the key sections most ATS and recruiters expect: Summary, Skills, Experience, and Education.

2. Formatting (20 points)

We flag things that commonly break ATS systems: tables, images, fancy fonts, headers/footers, multi-column layouts, and scanned PDFs.

3. Keywords (50 points)

When you paste a job description, we compare its keywords with your resume and score how closely they match (with more weight for real skills & tools).

4. Writing Quality (30 points)

We look for action verbs, achievements with numbers, weak phrases, repetition, and heavy passive voice to help you sound more impactful.
